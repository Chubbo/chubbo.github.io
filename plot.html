Prologue

◀ Previous Mission | Next Mission ▶

Part 1: The Robbery


When the mission begins, walk into the back office and intimidate the employees by pointing your weapon at them (DO NOT SHOOT THEM!) to make them shuffle into the back room. Whilst you are doing that, Trevor sets the charges on the vault door. Once he is back, use your phone’s remote function to detonate the charges.

Follow Trevor into the vault and collect the cash on the ground and in the back right corner.




Part 2: The Threat-Assessment


As you exit the vault, a security guard holds Michael up. From here, switch to Trevor by pressing and holding Down on the D-Pad and then use the Right Analog Stick to choose Trevor. Once the perspective changes, aim at the guard's head and execute him.



Part 3: The Escape


Follow the boys towards the exit and take cover as Brad detonates the doors. From there, take cover at the edge of the garage door and pick off the cops running towards you in the distance. Gradually advance from pillar to pillar, hugging cover. Cops will try to flank you on your left so watch out.



Part 3: The Getaway


Then step forward, running and gunning at the same time, (by not aiming with LT and only holding down the RT) go down the hill and then reach the getaway car and hop in. Be gentle on the wheel, and stay in the right lane to avoid an unfortunate collision with oncoming police or traffic. Stay on the road and don’t attempt any cross-country shortcuts or the mission will end.

Beat the train, and after the car crash cutscene, take cover and defend your fallen comrade.
After several cops are down, the mission is complete.

Franklin and Lamar

◀ Previous Mission | Next Mission ▶

Part 1: The Race


When the mission starts, walk over to the two cars in the back of the house and choose whichever one you like. From there, Lamar will trigger a short race, complete with several obstacles in the way. There are no checkpoint blips to follow, you will need to keep your eyes on Lamar as he zooms through traffic.

Use Franklin’s special wheelman ability to make the sharper turns. Doing this would enable time to go slow, but Franklins motion would still be razor sharp and help maneuver out of the sticky situations, and less of a chance of having a crash. Use Frank's ability several times to achieve the "Focused" requirement.

Continue to follow Lamar while steering clear of some of the obstacles that get in the way, especially vehicles that somehow block your way.

Part 2: Evading The Police

You will know that you won the race if Franklin arrives to the parking lot first. After you reach the drop point, you will be pursued by the police.

Evading them is relatively easy. a great hiding place would be under the freeway right behind the Vanilla Unicorn Strip Club (see screencap above). Wait for the wanted level to drop and continue on to Simeon's dealership.

When that is done, go outside and get into your car and drive back to the safehouse to complete the mission.

Part 1: The Drive

After the cutscene, exit the dealership and take one of the cars (preferably a fast one) and drive it to the waypoint at Vespucci Beach.

Complications

◀ Previous Mission | Next Mission ▶

Part 1: Breaking and Entering

When the mission begins, drive on over to Michael’s house. You can enter through the front gates by jumping over the entrance door or by jumping the fence at the tennis courts.

To enter the mansion, climb on top of the pickup truck, climb onto the roof, and then jump into the open window.

Switch into stealth mode, then make your way downstairs. Once you’re at the bottom of the stairs, sneak down the hall.

Part 2: The Drive Back

The garage entrance is just under the stairwell to your left. As long as the folks in the kitchen keep their backs to you everything will be fine. Sneak into the garage and jump in the car and drive back to the dealership.

Midway thru the trip, Michael pops in for a little surprise chat. Continue up to the dealership and follow Michael's demands to trigger the next section.

Part 3: The Fight

Control will shift to Michael. Beating up Simeon won’t be much of a challenge. Michael’s special ability allows him to slow down time, but even without that advantage an aggressive series of kicks and punches will bring the crooked car salesman down. Defeat Simeon to complete the mission.

Father Son

◀ Previous Mission | Next Mission ▶

Part 1: The Boat Chase

When the mission begins, get into Amanda’s convertible and drive towards the the lower beachside freeway. Once you get to the waypoint, Michael will spot the yacht, so chase after it!

Part 2: Freeway Shoot-out

Move close enough to allow Franklin on board. With fast driving skills, Franklin can board the boat just as Michael exits the tunnel.

After Franklin jumps aboard the boat, keep on the boat's tail and then start shooting at the baddies when Franklin gives the word. Press the L1/LT button to do drive-by shooting from the front window. When the coast is clear, Jimmy will end up dangling on the mast.

Part 3: The Car Repair

With Jimmy safe in the car, drive in close to the boat and catch Franklin. Get in close and a quick cutscene shows Franklin jumping off the boat to the car hood. From there, the engine gives out, so make a U-turn and drive to LS Customs to make some repairs.

After the cutscene, drive in to LS Customs and get the car fixed free of charge. Now that the car is fixed, drive it back to Michael's house.

Return to Michael’s house with Amanda's car in mint condition to complete the mission.

The Long Stretch

◀ Previous Mission | Next Mission ▶

Part 1: Shopping and Driving

After the cutscene, get into the vehicle and drive over to Ammu-Nation. Purchase the Shotgun (plus the Suppressor and some Body Armor if you have the extra paper) and then exit the store and drive to the meeting place.

Part 2: Escaping the Building

When you arrive at the place, make your way into the building and up the stairs to trigger a small gun battle with some gangsta baddies! Get into cover and start blasting at the baddies.

When the coast is clear, follow Lamar as they try to leave the building. Keep in mind that there are enemies at every corner so take cover! When you reach the stairway, carefully navigate down and a baddie will run up and shoot you, so blast him quickly.

When you reach the first floor, take cover behind the paper stacks when needed and keep moving forward and shooting. If you take to long, the Ballas set the area on fire, so stay mobile!

Navigate your way out of the building while keeping in cover. The exit doorway will have more baddies, so shoot them down and exit outside to find a helicopter in your way. Shoot it down to clear the area and then follow Lamar to the rooftops.

Part 2: Escaping the Police

Jump over the wall at the end of the rooftops and enter the vehicle and find a hideaway from the cops quickly! When the coast is clear, drive back to Frank's house to complete the mission.

Daddy's Little Girl

◀ Previous Mission | Next Mission ▶

Part 1: Bicycle Race

When the mission begins, get into the vehicle and drive to the waypoint at Vespucci Beach. Walk up to the nearby bicycle rental stand and choose any of the bikes to begin the race.

Bicycling is fairly straightforward. Tap the run button to pedal more rapidly, but watch out for Michael’s health. He’s not in great shape, and if you push too hard he’ll begin to lose health. That being said, Jimmy is much slower, so just pedal towards the pier from any direction.

After the race, Jimmy reveals the real reason for going to the beach. From there, jump into the water and swim towards the Dignity and climb aboard to trigger a cutscene.

Part 2: The Rescue

Once Tracey is aboard the jetski, gun it towards the tunnel just down the beach from the ship.

Part 3: The Escape

Follow the waterways going to the east side of the canals until you reach the marina area. Make a left turn at the marina and then go to the right towards the ocean. As you approach the ocean, make a U-turn at the small peninsula (see screencap above) and Michael will evade the baddies.

Once the baddies are gone, jet back to the beach and meet up with Jimmy to complete the mission.

Once you're stocked and primed, jump into Trevor's truck and drive over to the biker gang waypoint. From there, a short cutscene occurs followed by a chase!

Scouting the Port

◀ Previous Mission | Next Mission ▶

Part 1: The Drive to the Docks

When the mission begins, get into Trevor's truck and drive over to the LS Docks. Park the vehicle near the dispatch office and follow Floyd to his boss and then follow him thru the docks.

During the chat, a foreman will put Trevor and Floyd on a job moving containers from one section of the dock to another. Get into the large machine and drive towards the blue-colored containers that say "THRIFTEX".

Get in close and then lower the magnet and lift it up (press RIGHT on the D-Pad to make the container stick) and then drive it to the other side. Do it for both containers to complete this job.

Once that job is done, you’ll then move to the cargo crane. Climb the ladder and stairway and make it to the top of the crane where another foreman will ask for your help in moving containers from the back end to the front, where a awaiting truck is standing by.

Lower the crane and pick up the container and then zoom down towards the truck on the other end and place the container on it's bed. When done correctly the crane will automatically reset itself. Repeat for the second container to complete this job.

Part 2: Casing The Ship
